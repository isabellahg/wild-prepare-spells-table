Wild prepare spells

Temporary, unstable access to magic the sorcerer shouldn't fully be able to wield.

When

At the DM's discretion, the sorcerer's spell list may become unstable during any of:

Long rest Short rest Major story event Whenever appropriate
What changes

The sorcerer selects a number of their known spells. Those spells become unstable and are temporarily replaced by randomly rolled spells of the same level, drawn from a DM-approved Sorcerer or Wizard spell list or table.

Rules
  • Replacements count as Sorcerer spells and use Charisma as the casting modifier.
  • Each replacement occupies the same spell slot level as the spell it replaced.
  • Cannot exceed spell levels the sorcerer could normally cast.
  • Temporary — may change again after future rests or magical events.
The intent is not controlled optimization, but unstable access to magic the sorcerer should not fully be able to wield.
